Wellcome To Memory Capsule

This website hold and keeps our precious memories together with me.

The Idea

As more time you spend with me, I noticed that we can't save our photos in a single device forever. Therefore, I made a website just for that.

This website are a way of preserving a moment in our lives for the rest of our lives. Since the two of us, are not used to keep our photos in a flashy array of photo printers which can be lost or destroyed by time. It surely seems too common to use a flashdrive or an SD card in today's day and age. To our future selves, the innovation of storing photos in a website bringing about a new way of preserving our most precious memories. Not only do we have digital copies to keep with us for the rest of our days across hundreds of different archives.

What We Do?

Uploading each photos, even candid one's can be stored and will hold a sentimental value for us.

As each day passes on, every photo uploaded, and memory we have can be looked back upon. So stay forever with me as we explore different paths in our life and share what we experienced on what we go through in our career.
